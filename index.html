Types Of Courses

We offer different options for attending the courses. Whether it is remote or actually in our university, it doesn't matter, you can enroll quick and easy.

Online

You can attend an online course remotely from anywhere in the world. Online courses consist of special study plans, curriculums and weekly live lectures that you can also replay as a video. All study material is provided.

On-Campus

With On-Campus studying, you will be attending all your courses in our University alongside other students. Benefits include open libraries, gym, various sport activities, canteen, and more!

Online & On-Campus

The most dynamic option combines both remote and on-campus attendance. This Option provides you with freedom of choosing how to attend the course, so you can manage your time more efficiently.
